Samples

These are a selection of roller blind fabrics we have on hand. Most colours have multiple opacities available for different purposes.

The opacity rating measures how much light is let through. A larger number means more light. So a 1% fabric blocks more light than a 3% fabric which in turn blocks more light than a 5% fabric. Higher density fabrics provide greater temperature control and privacy.
